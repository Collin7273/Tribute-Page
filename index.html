Ken Block

  • 1967 - Ken Block is born on November 21 in Long Beach, California, USA.
  • 1994 - Co-founds DC Shoes, a skate apparel company.
  • 1998 - Begins competing in rally racing
  • 2005 - Makes his debut in the Rally America championship.
  • 2006 - Wins his first Rally America event at the Rally in the 100 Acre Wood.
  • 2008 - Makes his debut in the World Rally Championship.
  • 2010 - Launches the Gymkhana series of web videos, which showcase his precision driving skills.
  • 2011 - Co-founds Hoonigan Racing, a motorsport team and lifestyle brand.
  • 2012 - Finishes second in the Global Rallycross Championship.
  • 2014 - Releases Gymkhana 7, which becomes the most-watched motorsport video on YouTube.
  • 2016 - Wins the Global Rallycross Championship.
  • 2018 - Co-founds the Extreme E electric off-road racing series.
  • 2020 - Releases Gymkhana TEN, his most ambitious Gymkhana video yet.
  • 2023 - Dies in a snowmobile accident on January 2 in Wasatch County, Utah, USA.
